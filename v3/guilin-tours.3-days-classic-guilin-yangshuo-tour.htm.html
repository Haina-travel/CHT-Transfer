Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival and Classic Guilin
guilin yangshuo tour

Welcome to Guilin! No matter whether you’re arriving by plane or bullet train, your private guide will be ready to meet you at the arrival hall. A comfortable private car with an experienced driver will be waiting to show you the most beautiful sights of Guilin.

Let’s start with the symbol of Guilin, Elephant Trunk Hill. Take signature pictures, listen to the legend of how an elephant turned into a hill there, and find out the secrets hiding in the belly of this “elephant”.

After lunch, spend a literally cool afternoon in the Reed Flute Cave. It’s a pleasant temperature year-round and fun to see the amazing array of limestone formations highlighted in neon lights.

At the end of the day, your private guide and driver will transfer you to your hotel and assist you with check-in. Enjoy a relaxing evening.

Hotel options:

  • Shangri-La Hotel Guilin (best in Guilin)

Day 2 Li River Cruise and Xianggong Hill
xianggong hill

Karst hills, graceful bamboo groves, and fishing cormorants - the charming views of the Li River are the essence of Guilin. While most tour groups have a 3-star cruise, we will arrange a 4-star cruise (the highest level, with better facilities and buffet lunch) to ensure you have the best experience of the river's charm.

Arriving at Yangshuo, it’s time to explore a hidden gem of the Li River - Xianggong  Hill. It is considered to be one of the best places to enjoy a bird's eye view of the Li.

After the tour, sit back and relax, as our private transport returns you to your hotel in Guilin.
