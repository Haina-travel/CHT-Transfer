As the biggest island of Hong Kong, Lantau Island is full of fun: beautiful beaches, fishing villages, stunning mountain ranges, Giant Buddha, and elegant temples. Come and explore the best of it during our one-day tour.

Tour Highlights

  • Tour Name: One-Day Seat-in-Coach Tour to Lantau Island and Giant Buddha (no shopping tour)
  • Tour Code: HKSIC-6
  • Tour Duration: 6 hours
  • Highlights: Ngong Ping 360 cable car (round-trip), Po Lin Monastery, Buddha Museum, Tai O Fishing Village, stilt houses (a boat trip ride), Ngong Ping Village, vegetarian lunch
  • Departure Time: approx. 8:50am to 9:40am (times are subject to change due to local traffic conditions)

Suggested Itinerary

The tour coach will pick you up at the designated hotels. Please go to one of the hotels by yourself if you are not already staying at one of them. One day before departure, you will get a fax with detailed information about the collection time and place (and directions if there is no free collection from your hotel), the itinerary, sightseeing information, and emergency number of the tour operator in your hotel in Hong Kong.

Enjoy the good views as you are driven along Tsing Ma Bridge heading to Lantau Island. Visit one of Hong Kong's most fascinating sites and its principal center of Buddhism â€” Po Lin Monastery. Giant Buddha, which is the world's largest seated outdoor bronze Buddha, is situated on the mountain peak. A round-trip Ngong Ping 360 cable car ride is included for you to have a nice view of the Buddha.


After a vegetarian lunch at the monastery, stop at the quaint and picturesque village of Tai O, and enjoy a sampan ride around the stilt houses. Drop by Ngong Ping Village, a culture-themed village with lively tour experiences.

After the tour, you will be returned back to the hotel where you were collected from.

Trip Notes

1. About the Booking

Confirmation will be received within one working day, unless booked within 5 days of travel. In this case, confirmation will be received within two working days, subject to availability.

2. Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car

This tour is subject to the weather conditions and the operation of the cable car.
If the cable car is stopped before the tour starts, the tour will be cancelled.
If the cable car is stopped during the tour, other public transportation will be used.
