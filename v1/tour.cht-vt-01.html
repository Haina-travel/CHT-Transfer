Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 Siem Reap Arrival

Upon arrival in Siem Reap, you will be warmed by your tour guide's smile. Transfer to hotel and free at leisure.

- Entering Cambodia:
1. Bangkok can be an easy gateway to fly to Siem Reap. Extend your China trip to Cambodia? Guangzhou has direct flight.
2. Apply for visa-on-arrival in Siem Reap: you will need 2 passport-photo-sized photos, a valid passport and USD30 in cash.

Accommodation
3 nights at Tara Angkor Hotel (4-star) / Angkor Village Hotel (5-star)

Day 2 Siem Reap Angkor Wat Wonder

The famous Angkor Wat Complex consists of three major parts and the most important one is the central loop that is made of Angkor Wat, Angkor Thom, Bayon Temple and Ta Prohm. Today your local tour guide will take you for a deep visit at this section.

Angkor Wat is the main site of the temple structure; Angkor Thom used to be the king's palace; Bayon Temple is widely known for the mysterious smile, Smile of Angkor; Ta Prohm, a must-see for movie fans of Tomb Raider and travelers alike. All of these four sites are different in styles and building purposes yet one thing in common is that they stand for the essence of Angkorian arts.

Day 3 Siem Reap Less-Seen Highlights

Most travel groups would put Bantey Srei as a backup option yet today you will see your trip is perfected with a visit to this sparking pearl of Angkor. The entire temple was built with pink sandstones and the carvings are nothing but esquisite.

Learn about Mr. Aki Ra's life in Cambodia Landmine Museum. He was raised to plant bombs during the time of Khmer Rouge and now he's dedicating his life doing the opposite: removing bombs throughout the contry. Next stop is the less-seen site of Beng Meala, where giant thress stretch out from giant rocks and scattered stones of the old temples.
Tonight, you will enjoy an Apsara Dance over dinner.

Day 4 Siem Reap Fly to Ho Chi Minh City

After breakfast, have some free time in the city. Fly to Ho Chi Minh City and free at leisure for the rest of today.

Accommodation
3 nights at Parkroyal Saigon Hotel (4-star)/ Grand Hotel Saigon (5-star)

Day 5 Ho Chi Minh City Highlights

Formerly known as Saigon, Ho Chi Minh City welcomes every traveler with undying passion. Today you will visit its highlights including Reunification Palace , Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Central Post Office, before stopping for a coffee at a local caf√©. After lunch, explore local's daily life at Chinatown and Binh Tay Market .

- Motorbike option:
For group with less than 5 people, we would recommend an option by motorbike. Local rider (your tour guide) can take you to some back alleys that big travel groups cannot get in.

Day 6 Ho Chi Minh City Cu Chi Tunnels and Fly to Danang, Transfer to Hoi An

After breakfast, transfer to the famous site of Cu Chi Tunnels. Back in wartime, the Viet Cong dug a web of tunnels as a battle system against the American and today you will have a great chance to see what's like underneath.

Ttransfer to the airport and fly to Danang. Transfer to Hoi An within 40 minutes. Free at leisure.

Accommodation
2 nights at Essence Hoi An Hotel (4-star)/ Palm Garden Beach Resort & Spa(5-star)

Day 7 Hoi An Ancient Town

Listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hoi An Ancient Town has a lot of stories to tell. Today you will walk around this delightful town with your local tour guide, visiting the Japanese Covered Bridge, Tran Family Chapel, Fukian Assembly Hall and the Old House of Tan Ky. Learn to make paper lanterns after sightseeing. Free time on your own afterwards.

Day 8 Hoi An Free Time, Transfer to Danang and Fly to Hanoi

Enjoy some free time in Hoi An. Later, transfer to Danang and fly to the capital Hanoi. Check in and have the rest of today free at leisure.

Accommodation
1 night at Hanoi Pearl Hotel (4-star)/ Apricot Hotel Hanoi(5-star)

Day 9 Hanoi to Halong Bay

Halong Bay is 3-hour driving away from Hanoi. The drive is tasteless yet the cruise experience is all worthy.

Itineraries differ for each cruise, but you will probably visit: Cua Van Fishing Village, Vung Vieng Fishing Village, Me Cung Caves, Virgin Cave, and Surprise Cave, as well as passing some small islets, such as Flight Cock Islet, Human Head Islet, and Toad Islet and etc. Some cruises also include a cooking class at night and a taichi lesson in the morning.

Accommodation
1 nights on Halong Bay Paloma Cruise (4-star)/ Paradise Luxury Cruise (5-star)

Day 10 Halong Bay to Hanoi, Hanoi Free Time

Finish your cruise in the afternoon. Transfer back to Hanoi and enjoy some free time.

Accommodation
2 nights at Hanoi Pearl Hotel (4-star)/ Apricot Hotel Hanoi(5-star)

Day 11 Hanoi City Tour

Hanoi is the witness of Vietnam's history and today you will share its insight. Today's city tour will take you to an interesting museum outside the city: Vietnam Museum of Ethnology. Later, learn the mind-provoking history at Hoa Lo Prison before lunch at a local restaurant.

At Ho Chi Minh Presidential Palace Historical Site, your private tour guide will tell you stories about Uncle Ho. Later, walk around Hoan Kiem Lake and the Old Quarter before the last stop at Dong Xuan Market.

Day 12 Hanoi Fly to Luang Prabang

Fly to Luang Prabang and have the rest of today free at leisure.

Accommodation
2 nights at Belle Rive Boutique Hotel (4-star)/ Satri House Secret Retreat(5-star)

Day 13 Luang Prabang Cultural Tour

The peaceful town of Luang Prabang enamors its travelers with the poetry-like tranquility and Buddhism is their answer of life. Today you will feel Luang Prabang's spirit, visiting Xieng Thong Temple, the Traditional Art Exhibition Center and the Royal Palace Museum. At the evening, visit a local market to see what people prepare for their dinner.

- Sunset at Phousi Mountain:
Walk up to the top of Phousi Mountain and get a nice view of the town at sunset.
