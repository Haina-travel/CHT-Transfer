Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 Shanghai to Beijing, the Great Wall at Mutianyu

Meals: lunch

Accommodation: Sunworld Hotel Beijing (4-star)/ Beijing Marriott Hotel City Wall (5-star)

You will start the tour with an early flight from Shanghai Pudong Airport to Beijing. Upon arrival, meet your tour guide and begin your Beijing Exploration!

Drive 1½ hours from the airport to the Great Wall at Mutianyu. Your guide will introduce to you the history and cultural background of Beijing city and the Great Wall.

First have lunch at the Xin Shuang Quan Restaurant, which is near the Great Wall. This garden-style restaurant serves farmhouse dishes, specializing in rainbow trout. The fabulous taste will leave you with great memories.

The Mutianyu section of the Great Wall is the less crowded section with beautiful scenery, and you will take a cable car to save time and energy. Have a relaxing walk on the Wall and enjoy the beautiful afternoon sunshine.

Shanghai to Beijing Transportation:

  • We recommend a flight from Shanghai to Beijing, which takes about 2 hours. If you like, we can also arrange a bullet train (about 5½ hours).
  • We will book the flight or train in advance for you, you just need to arrive at the airport or train station on time.
Day 2Forbidden City, Summer Palace, back to Shanghai

Meals: breakfast & lunch

Today you will visit 2 of the most important attractions in Beijing city, the Forbidden City and the Summer Palace.

First arrive at the biggest public square in the world – Tian'anmen Square. Your tour guide will introduce the nearby buildings, such as the National Grand Theater and the Chairman Mao Memorial Hall, and leave time for you to take photos.

Walk across the road and you will arrive at the Forbidden City. Have a 2½ hour stroll and explore the royal palace where 24 Chinese emperors worked and lived. You will learn a lot of history and hear some interesting stories about Chinese emperors.

Have lunch at the Courtyard 7 Restaurant, which is located in the hutong area. Here you can not only taste delicious gourmet food, but also enjoy a piece of unsullied land, a haven in such a busy city.

In the afternoon have a walk around the royal garden – the Summer Palace. This is the place where emperors lived in the summer time to escape from the heat. Walk through the world's longest corridor and take a boat on the lake. Enjoy a relaxing afternoon.

After the Summer Palace, your driver and guide will escort you to Beijing Capital Airport and check you onto the flight back to Shanghai.
