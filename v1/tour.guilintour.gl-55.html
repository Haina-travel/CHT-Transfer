Tour Highlights

  • Bike among the most beautiful karst hills area of Guilin
  • Enjoy a peaceful and relaxed countryside in Yangshuo
  • Have fun with a local family visiting

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 Yanghsuo Leisure Biking and Local Family Meeting (2-2.5h biking)

Your private guide will pick up you from your hotels in Yangshuo, and transfer you to Yangzhou’s Meeting Dragon River. Meeting Dragon River and the Li River are the most famous and beautiful rivers with numerous karst hills in Yangshuo. You will bike along one of the best sections of Meeting Dragon River (From Jima to Yangshuo). Usually, it will take around 2-2.5 hours to cycle depending on your physical conditions and cycling skills. During the biking, you will visit a local family to know more about China.

In the afternoon, get ready to learn how to cook Chinese cuisine, have an opportunity to learn three dishes and enjoy the fruits of your labor. Stay in a 3-star hotel on the West Street, enjoy the vivid night of foreign street.

Good to know: West Street in Yangshuo is also called the Foreign Street because a lot of foreigners come there. It’s famous for its bars and vivid night life in the evening.

Day 2Biking from Yangshuo to Aishan to Puyi, back to Yangshuo

This is a full day of in-depth bicycle tour. Experience the hustle & bustle of the Yangshuo traffic for a few minutes before you escape onto the country roads, lanes and tracks of the distinctive and beautiful Yangshuo countryside. You will visit two villages; the first is Aishan, and the second is Puyi. Both have a simple and unsophisticated culture as well as beautiful natural scenery. It is an opportunity to see real country life and talk to the local famers. Later, cycle back to your hotel in Yangshuo.
