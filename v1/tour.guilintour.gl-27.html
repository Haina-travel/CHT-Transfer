Longji Terraced Fields is a must-hiking destination in Guilin. Proper hiking along the terraced fields and mountains offer you a chance to immerse yourself at the the fields , get a touch with local Zhuang people and their culture, taste local unique food like oil-tea, have fun with peaceful countryside life.

Note: The price is based on your hotel in Guilin city center.

Tour Highlights

  • Unique hiking along the terraced fields
  • Get closely touch with the local and their life
  • Take photos of local minority houses with the fields
  • Taste Zhuang People's unique food-oil tea

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 Enjoy the Best View of Dazhai Village(2-3h hiking)

Your English guide will pick you up from your hotel at Guilin in the morning. Private car and driver will take you to Longji Terraced Fields with a 2-2.5h journey. You will go through Dazhai Village, the best part of Longji with more magnificent terraced fields but fewer travelers. The village is impressive of a large-scale wooden minority houses, especially decorated by lots of hot red peppers and golden rice plants in autumn.

Have fun to chat with local Zhuang people at their home, and taste local unique food-oil tea at noon. You can also learn how to make the oil tea with them who have lived there for generations. Start your hiking up to mountain ridge for a better view and reach your hotel. The hiking lasts 2-3 hours to your hotel which has a panorama view of the fields. Come cross some local people working in the fields, enjoy a beautiful picture with local wooden houses decorated by terraced fields, walk through the fantastic fields-a golden heaven in autumn.

Day 2 Enjoy Hiking to Ping’an Village, Back to Guilin (4-5h hiking)

Have a local Chinese breakfast at the hotel in the morning. Leave your hotel at 08:00, and start a longer hiking which is 4-5h hiking to Ping’an village. You will walk down and go uphill among the mountains. Guided by local people, enjoy a peaceful countryside life, and have a look at ethnic people life.

Later, arrive at Ping’an village-a popular destination in the Longji Terraced Fields. Walk down from the top with the best view, have lunch in Ping’an village and try the local bamboo rice. Continue to explore the terraced fields after the meal, and start to move back to Guilin.

Trip Notes

1. Best time to do the hiking

May to October is the best period to hike among the terraced fields. Avoid Guilin’s monsoon (March and April).

2. What to see

Watering the fields starts every April, planting in June and harvest comes during late September. You could find a special panoramic view from May to September, especially for photograph lovers. Your tour guide will gladly lead you to the best spots with panoramic view.

Golden terraced fields are fabulous during China’s National Day holiday. Although we don’t recommend visiting China during the period, yet it’s the best time to see and photograph the countless yellow rice terraces. The rice stalks don’t turn golden before National Day and is quickly cut after the holiday. Rice in Dazhai Village is cut later than Ping’an Village area.

3. What to pack

The route is touched by a few travelers. Bring some water and snacks while there is only one shop on the way from Dazhai village to Ping’an village (4-5h hiking). Good walking shoes are necessary.
