1 Day Private Yangmingshan Volcano & Beitou Hot Spring Tour

tw-07: Yangmingshan volcano & Beitou hot spring tour
Nature

Explore Yangmingshan National Park, an extinct volcanic terrain park that is regarded as Taipei's Backyard Garden. On this full-day tour take your chance to visit historical buildings, verdant plains, beautiful grass fields and the tranquil hot springs of Beitou.

Tour Highlights

  • Chung-Shan Building, the building featured on the back side of the 100 TWD bill
  • Sunshine-filled grassy meadows along Valley of the Sunl
  • Relaxing by the tranquil hot spring at Thermal Valley
  • Tudor-style Beitou Museum of hot springs' culture & history

Suggested Itinerary

Depart from your hotel and enjoy an hour-long scenic ride to Yangmingshan National Park. Upon arrival, take photos of this breathtaking park with gorgeous crater lakes and sulfur fumaroles. Your first stop is Chung-Shan Building, which is featured on the reverse of the New Taiwan $100 bill and was designed by a female architect. Here, marvel at its white walls and green tiles styled after the traditional Chinese court.

Have a break for a delicious lunch before making your way to the verdant Qingtiangang Meadow, also known as the Valley of the Sun. Breathe in the fresh mountain air as you take a relaxing walk with views of sunshine-filled grassy meadows and cattle grazing in the pastures. Then head to the Thermal Valley(closed on Monday), a remnant of the park's former volcanic activity, where the spring's waves of hot air and a cool breeze make you feel like you are in a sauna.From here, head to Beitou Hot Spring Museum(closed on Monday), one of the largest public baths in East Asia. Be mesmerized at its Victorian-style building with red bricks, wooden weatherboard, and black tile roof set perfectly against the surrounding greenery. While here, learn about Beitou's hot spring culture and history before your transfer takes you back to your hotel.
