Alshan's advantageous geographic position gives the city many fantastic and natural tourist attractions, including the Alshan National Forest Park.. This great natural scenery has helped the city become a newborn tourist attraction in China. 

Alshan National Forest Park

Alshan National Forest Park is located at the southwest foot of Inner Mongolian side of Daxinganlin. It covers an area of 103000 square meters. Alshan National Forest Park is the ideal summer resort due to it..
