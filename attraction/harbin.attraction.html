Harbin’ attractions are more related to snow and ice, because the area is covered in ice and snow during the winter. If you are a snow lover, Harbin could be a great tourist destination for you: Yabuli Skiing Center and Jihua Ski Resort bring you the joy of skiing and Ice and Snow World, Sun Island Scenic Spot, and the annual Harbin International Ice and Snow Festival let you enjoy ice sculptures.
