Yichang attractions are dominated by the Three Gorges, with 8 of the 12 attractions listed on the Yangtze River. From Yichang you could take a Yangtze cruise upriver towards Chongqing through the gorges or downstream to Wuhan and Shanghai. The giant dams: the Three Gorges Dam and Gezhou Dam, along with the towering gorges: Xiling Gorge, Wu Gorge, and Qutang Gorge, make up a spectacular array of sights.

There is also a sturgeon museum, historic residences, temples, and ancient architecture in and around the city.
